Ciao, my name is Gessica. I'm a full stack Web Developer living in London.

He advised me to give coding a go just to see if I would be interested in it. I attempted a code challenge online and that birthed my interest in coding. To improve my coding skills I carried out the Web Developement Immersive Course with General Assembly where I gained a great understanding of full stack developement and I now love what I do.
